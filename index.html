About Me

Before I transitioned to a life of coding, my <non-tech> career path was built out of service to others through roles in customer service and HR. Now as a developer, I get to marry those people-oriented skills to create clean, functional websites that are beautiful to look at and easy to navigate.

I'm constantly expanding my expertise in how I design and build websites. I'm currently adding ReactJS and Bootstrap to my growing library of skills that includes: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery, Rest APIs, Mobile and Responsive Web Design, GIT/GitHub, VS Code, Adobe Photoshop, and CodeSandbox.

I’m an Enneagram 1, so I can be a bit of a perfectionist 😁; but only because I’ve always looked for ways to improve what’s been in front of me - processes, communication, culture, and now, websites.

Outside of the coding life I enjoy playing the guitar and making music, building/tinkering with things, or a fun and possibly over-competitive board game with the family. Speaking of, I have the pleasure of chasing around seven awesome and rambunctious kiddos with my lovely and all too patient wife Amber.

Let’s build something great together!
